# TRAIDE

**The cybernetic AI DeFi engine. Multi-chain ecosystem with the trade token at the core. Pronounced "trade" (the AI part is in the name, not in your mouth).**

[traidedefi.com](https://traidedefi.com)

---

## What it is

TRAIDE is a cybernetic engine wrapping AI execution around DeFi primitives across multiple chains. The flywheel: cross-chain liquidity routing feeds the AI execution layer; the AI layer surfaces trade opportunities the routing layer can act on; the trade token captures the value of the loop.

Multi-chain by design (not as a marketing line, as the architectural premise). The AI layer is the differentiator: most DeFi tools route, TRAIDE decides.

TRAIDE is pronounced "trade" (one syllable, like the English word). The trade token is referenced as "the trade token" in speech, never as "T-R-A-I-D-E."
